ABOUT:


Yoga for All studio was opened in 2019 in Nuremberg, Germany with the belief of TRANSFORMATION. Whether you are a beginner or someone who wants to deepen your practice, we are committed to bring about a transformation within you through yoga. Yoga for all welcomes adults, children(above 4 years) and expecting mothers to a space spread across 200qm, where you can exercise, meditate, relax and learn. We are followers of Vinyasa Yoga (which focuses on slow and controlled movements) and Hatha Yoga (which is more fast paced and can account for a good cardio session).


The trainers work whole-heartedly and provide special attention and guidance to children in their yoga practice. Each session ends with a 5-minute guided meditation and breath-work that leaves you feeling relaxed and rejuvenated.


Yoga benefits:

Yoga includes gentle exercise, breath control and meditation. Regular practice of yoga has health benefits that include lowering BP, improved posture and circulation, weight-loss, increased flexibility, and a sense of well-being.


For Whom:

Yoga is for everyone, be it prenatal yoga for moms-to-be or new moms who are looking for getting back into exercise, yoga for children, and others. You can choose the form of yoga based on your individual needs. Classes are conducted in small groups so that trainers can provide attention and help you practice the asanas with correct posture.
